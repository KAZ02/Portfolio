【Oigawa Railway】
Oigawa Railway is the only railroad in Japan where you can climb the steepest gradient in Japan with an apt-system locomotive. You can enjoy the view of the emerald green Oigawa River, the dam with its suspension bridge, and the railway bridge as you ride along. This is a recommended tourist spot for people who like trains, dams, and nature.
